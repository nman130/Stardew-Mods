Menus Everywhere - 0.1.1

This mod allows players to access various menus from anywhere in the game.

Requirements:
Stardew Valley Version 1.4
SMAPI Version 3.5.0

Accessible Menus & Default Keybinds:
Calendar - F1
Wanted Board - F2
Monster Eradication Board - F3
Shipping Bin - F4

Accessing bounties from outside the community center can be turned off in the config.
Accessing the shipping bin from outside the farm can be turned on in the config (it is off by default).

Warning: Completing rooms from outside the community center will corrupt your save.
